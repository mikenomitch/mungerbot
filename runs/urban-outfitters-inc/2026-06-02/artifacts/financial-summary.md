- **ROIC vs WACC**: NOPAT FY2026 ≈ $472M (op. income × (1−22%)). Including capitalized leases in invested

  ROIC has roughly **tripled off the FY2022 trough**.
- ROE driven primarily by rising **net margin**, not leverage (leverage actually fell 2.17→1.78 as equity compounded and the company stayed debt-free).
