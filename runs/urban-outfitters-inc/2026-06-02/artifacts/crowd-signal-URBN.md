### Where the crowd disagrees with itself (the key cross-check)
1. **Sell-side bullish (Moderate Buy, ~$87, +20%, zero sells) vs. short sellers leaning in (~12% float, rising MoM, ~6-day cover).** Two informed crowds on opposite sides.
2. **Muted +3% reaction to a +16% EPS beat** says expectations were already high — the bullish thesis is partly *in the price*, aligning with the shorts' "priced for perfection / fashion mean-reversion" worry more than with the targets' +20% upside.
3. **Cheap multiple (fwd P/E ~11.6) vs. elevated short interest** is the tension downstream lanes should resolve: is the low multiple a value opportunity (sell-side view) or the market's discount for cyclical/peak-earnings risk after 7 straight record quarters (short view)?
