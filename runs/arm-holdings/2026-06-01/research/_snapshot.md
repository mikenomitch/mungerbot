## Context flags for downstream lanes
- **Valuation is rich on any conventional metric.** A ~$300B+ market cap against ~$1.5B FY2026 operating cash flow implies an extremely high multiple of cash flow/earnings. The valuation and earnings-quality lanes should foreground this; the "default is PASS / Buy must be earned" discipline is especially relevant here.
- **Free float is thin.** SoftBank owns ~87% of issued share capital and the bulk of voting power, so the public float is small (~13%), which can amplify price volatility and weakens minority-shareholder governance leverage. The leadership/governance and supply-demand lanes should weigh this.
- **Operating-cash-flow figure (~$1.524B FY2026)** comes from secondary reporting of Arm's FY2026 results (FYE 2026-03-31); the financials lane should pull the primary 20-F / 6-K to confirm revenue, royalties vs. licensing mix, GAAP vs. non-GAAP EPS, and the precise cash/investments balance.
