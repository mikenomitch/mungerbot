## 6. Where crowd and fundamentals disagree (for downstream lanes)

1. **Tape vs. sell-side:** price $408.85 vs. avg target ~$227–241 ⇒ analysts see ~40%+ downside even after raising numbers. The market is pricing a more aggressive AGI-CPU/data-center ramp than the modeled consensus.
2. **Momentum vs. valuation:** 163x forward earnings, 73x forward sales, PEG 5.3 — the crowd is paying a momentum/AI-optionality premium that no conventional multiple supports.
3. **Event vs. drift:** the May earnings *beat* sold off 7% (supply-cap + smartphone-unit warning), yet the stock then doubled on a *partner's* product launch (Nvidia) — sentiment is being set by ecosystem narrative, not ARM's own guidance (which mgmt declined to raise on supply constraints).
4. **Tail risk:** ~24% crowd-implied AI-bubble-burst-by-2026 is the cleanest external "what could break the thesis" gauge.
