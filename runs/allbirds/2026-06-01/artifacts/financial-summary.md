> **Reading note.** Allbirds reports a **single operating segment** (footwear/apparel,
> DTC + wholesale + international distributors). FY2025 and FY2026 figures reflect a
> business in terminal decline that is being **sold for $39M** to American Exchange
> Group (close expected Q2 2026); the surviving public shell is pivoting to a
> **GPU-leasing "Electronics Infrastructure Business."** See NOTES.md for the
> transformation detail — the historical statements below describe the *legacy*
> footwear company, most of which is leaving the entity.
